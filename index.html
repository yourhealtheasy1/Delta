FAQs About Delta Air Lines

What are Delta SkyMiles and how does it work?

Delta SkyMiles is Delta’s frequent flyer program where you earn miles on every flight. Miles can be used for upgrades, free tickets or other benefits. The more you fly Delta or its partners the more miles you earn.

Can I change or cancel my Delta flight after I book?

Yes, Delta is flexible with its bookings. You can change or cancel your flight but fees may apply depending on your ticket type. During the booking process you can select tickets that offer more flexibility.

Does Delta have in-flight Wi-Fi?

Yes, most Delta planes have Wi-Fi. You can buy Wi-Fi or if you’re a SkyMiles member you can take advantage of offers that may include free or discounted Wi-Fi.

How do I check in for my Delta flight?

You can check in for your Delta flight online through their website or mobile app 24 hours before your flight. Or you can check in at the airport using self-service kiosks or Delta ticket counters.

What are Delta’s baggage policies?

Delta’s baggage policy allows one carry-on and one personal item (e.g. purse or laptop bag) per passenger free of charge. Checked baggage fees vary based on your ticket type and destination. SkyMiles Medallion members and Delta Amex cardholders may be eligible for free checked baggage.

What is Delta Comfort+?

Delta Comfort+ is an upgraded seat that offers more legroom, dedicated overhead bin space and premium snacks. Passengers also get to board earlier and get complimentary drinks on select flights.

How do I get into the Delta Sky Club?

You can join the Delta Sky Club by purchasing an annual membership or using certain Delta credit cards that offer Sky Club access. SkyMiles Medallion members may also be eligible based on their status.

Can I bring my pet on board?

Yes, Delta allows small pets such as cats, dogs and household birds in the cabin for a fee as long as they meet certain requirements. Pets must be in an approved carrier that fits under the seat in front of you.

Are there special meals on Delta flights?

Yes, Delta offers special meals including vegetarian, kosher, halal and gluten-free options. Special meal requests must be made at least 24 hours before your flight.

Delta Air Lines Additional Informations

  • Delta's History: Delta was founded in 1929 as Huff Daland Airways. We started with one de Havilland DH-4 biplane. In 1930 Huff Daland Airways was renamed Delta Air Service. Delta Air Service started scheduled passenger service in 1931. In 1937 Delta Air Service was renamed Delta Air Lines. Delta Air Lines grew throughout the 20th century. In 1960 Delta Air Lines was the first to fly jets between the US and Europe. In 1972 Delta Air Lines was the first to offer nonstop service between the US and Asia. Delta Air Lines has continued to grow and expand in the 21st century. In 2008 Delta Air Lines merged with Northwest Airlines. Delta Air Lines is now one of the biggest airlines in the world.
  • Delta's Hubs and Destinations: Delta has hubs at Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport, Detroit Metropolitan Wayne County Airport, John F. Kennedy International Airport, Los Angeles International Airport, Minneapolis-Saint Paul International Airport, and Seattle-Tacoma International Airport. Delta serves over 350 destinations in 52 countries on 6 continents.
